About This Map

The purpose of this map is to share the Grinnell Journal I produced in the course of my dissertation research and other trips. The map uses Knight Lab's StoryMap tools. This is very much a work in progress

The full text of the journal entries published in my dissertation are available here:

Tobias, M.M. 2012. California Beach Plant Biogeography: Biogeomorphology and Ecological Succession, Effects of Foot-Traffic Concentration, and Low-Altitude Aerial Photography. Dissertation. University of California, Davis. Online
